Ameen ul Haq is a determined and technically sound defender from Kasaragod, Kerala. At just 17 years old, he already displays the hallmarks of a seasoned defender — reading the game intelligently, making timely interceptions, winning aerial duels, and organizing his backline with confidence.

Inspired by the relentless work ethic and drive of Cristiano Ronaldo, Ameen approaches every training session and match with the mentality of becoming the best version of himself. His hometown of Ethirthode may be small, but his ambitions reach far beyond.
